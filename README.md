## About me

- QA Engineer at **Nina Tecnologia**, a healthtech SaaS platform for clinics and hospitals.
- Uncommon path: **4 years in tech support**, **3 years as a UX/freelance designer**, now focused on **Quality**. That mix means I test with a real eye for the user, not just the checklist.
- Based in the **Florianópolis area, SC, Brazil**. Software Engineering background.
- I don't just report bugs: I build **NinaDash** (Jira metrics dashboard), the **NinaFlow** chatbot-flow generator and studio, and small tools that make QA and daily life easier.
- Studying towards **ISTQB CTFL** and leveling up **English** for international work.
